Beaulah's Bakery

It's not a treat without wheat and yeast!

Beaulah's Bakery has proudly been a family-owned and operated business since 1998. We serve our community a variety of fresh baked breads and pastries daily, with recipies handed down through generations of Beaulahs. We hope to see you soon!

About Us

Locally Sourced

At Beaulah's Bakery, we work with local growers and farmers to source all of our fruit, eggs, milk, and butter. Come taste the difference that a farm-fresh egg makes in a freshly baked loaf of challah bread. Local sourcing isn't just the right thing to do, it's the delicious thing to do!

Family Owned

Beaulah McGillicuddy grew up in a family of bakers. Whether it was baking cookies with grandma or a birthday cake with mom for her brothers, she was a natural at what she did. She fell in love with the craft and started Beaulah's Bakery to showcase her baking prowess through decades-old family tradition and a whole lot of love.

Giving Back

At Beaulah's Bakery, it's important to us that we give back to the community that makes us who we are. That's why, for each dollar you spend, we donate ten cents to local shelters and youth programs. We believe that we are here to serve our community, not the other way around.
